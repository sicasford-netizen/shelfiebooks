Why ShelfieBooksBooks

Everything a reader needs. Nothing a reader doesn't.

Add a book in seconds

Scan the barcode or search by title — your book lands on your shelf before you've found your reading glasses.

Three tidy shelves

Reading, Read, and Want to Read. The same way you'd sort them at home, minus the dusting.

Sign in, simply

Continue with Google or Apple. Fast to set up, private by design — your shelf is yours alone.

Always with you

Your shelf syncs across devices, so it's there at the bookshop, the library, or in the queue for coffee.

How it's organised

Three shelves. That's it.

Other apps give you stats, streaks, and someone else's opinions. ShelfieBooksBooks gives you a quiet, well-kept shelf — like your favourite independent bookshop, but it fits in your pocket.
